instead of having 2 messages per day, change it to a single message each day, updated in the morning only. introduce a new nuggets tab, that aims to share fun facts and latest tech trends.

## nugget contents
- 1 nugget for a random fun fact
    - fun fact should be something that is not commonly known, but will be interesting for people to know
    - it will be good if the fun fact is relatable, but an interesting fact works too
- 1 nugget for the a recent trend in tech
    - this can be a big tech breakthrough
    - the focus should be AI and ML, but generic tech breakthroughs that are ground-breaking will be good too
    - the tech nugget does not need to have been released on that day itself, but just something that has been trending in recent days or weeks

## generation mechanics
- generation of nuggets should be dynamic as far as possible, similar to the messages
    - web search or crawling of certain tech news or research paper sites may be needed
    - this should be done as efficiently as possible, and any sources or APIs used should be free to use
- updating of nuggets should be together in the same github action as the messages and doodle prompts


## nugget display
- the nuggets should exist on a separate tab, just like messages and doodles
- design a simple UI to display both the nuggets, maintaining the simplistic theme of the app, yet having some distinction between the fun fact and the tech trend
